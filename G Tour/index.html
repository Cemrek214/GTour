Welcome To İstanbul

Welcome to G Bosphorus Tour. We are happy to serve you. Our professional team has been working in this sector for years and our top priority is to make your family or friends have a great time. Are you ready to go on an unforgettable journey with your family or friends?

Our tour starts at 20.30 and ends at 11.15. We have shuttles that pick up our guests from their hotels and drop them off at their hotels. We pick up our valued guests from their hotels between 19-19.30 and when the tour is over, we direct you to your vehicles and drop you off at your hotels safely. We deal with any problems or questions our valued customers may have during the tour.

Dear guests, when you come to the ship and sit at your tables, we will leave you alone with our appetizers and salad plates and you can enjoy your unlimited drinks throughout the tour. Afterwards, we will serve you the main course you want from our chicken meatballs and fish options. Our professional waiters will be there for your every request. Finally, we offer you dessert.

While you enjoy your food and drinks, our folk dance team will be on stage to perform the traditional Turkish dances that we have prepared for you. Our animator will sing songs with you and finally, we will leave the stage to you with a DJ performance.
